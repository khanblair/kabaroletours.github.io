About Us

Background


Kabarole Tours and Safari Ltd. is a highly commendable and unique eco-tourism organisation founded by the former primary school teacher Richard Tooro in 1992. Since then the organisation has become a driving force in nature conservation and in various community development projects around Fort Portal and the Rwenzori region.

Kabarole Tours caters to the needs of travelers and offers a variety of tours that will give clients the experience of a lifetime and unforgettable memories through interactions with local people, activities and nature tours.

Kabarole Tours


With a strong commitment to eco-tourism and sustainability, Kabarole Tours and Safari Ltd. continues to make a positive impact on nature conservation and community development. Led by Richard Tooro, a former primary school teacher, the organization has been instrumental in supporting local communities in the Fort Portal and Rwenzori region.

At Kabarole Tours, we prioritize the needs of our travelers, offering a diverse range of tours that provide unparalleled experiences that satistfy their needs.
