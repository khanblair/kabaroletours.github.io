Our Portfolio

Kibale National Park Safari

Explore the enchanting Kibale National Park, renowned for its abundant primate population, including the playful chimpanzees. Witness the primates in their natural habitat, spot a diverse array of birdlife, and immerse yourself in the park's lush, verdant landscapes. The safari typically lasts 2-3 days and is rated from easy to moderate, with the main highlights being chimpanzee trekking and guided nature walks.

Cultural Immersion Tour

Dive into the rich cultural tapestry of the Fort Portal region, engaging with local communities, exploring traditional villages, and gaining insights into the vibrant heritage and customs of the Toro Kingdom. The cultural immersion tour lasts 1-2 days and is rated as easy, with highlights including visits to local artisan workshops, traditional dance performances, and learning about the Toro Kingdom's history.

Crater Lake Hike

Embark on a captivating hike around the serene and scenic Crater Lakes, marveling at the breathtaking vistas and learning about the unique geological formations that have shaped this magnificent landscape. The hike can be completed in 1-2 days and is considered easy, offering stunning crater lake views and opportunities for swimming and relaxation.

Additional Information

Our tours accommodate small to medium-sized groups, ensuring a personalized and intimate experience. We offer a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly lodges to luxury eco-camps, to suit your preferences and budget. Kabarole Tours is committed to responsible and sustainable tourism practices, minimizing our environmental impact and supporting local communities. We are also happy to work with you to create a personalized itinerary that caters to your specific interests and travel preferences.
