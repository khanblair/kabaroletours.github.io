Services

Permit Reservation

We can arrange for you the booking of Chimp or Gorilla Permits. You will need a permit if you would like to track the chimpanzees in Kibale National Park or the mountain gorillas in Bwindi National Park. Our service fee for booking the chimp permit is 15$ per permit and 40$ for a gorilla permit. Our service fee is additional to the costs of the permit. A gorilla permit is 700$ all year round. A chimp permit costs 200$, chimp habituation experience is 250$ and Gorilla habituation is now at 1500$ per permit per person.

Vehicle Hire


Our well trained drivers and tour guides will take you wherever you would like to go all over Uganda. We have vehicles of different sizes, types (2x2/4x4) and accompanying equipment like a convertible roof if you’re in for game drives. Booking yourself a vehicle will mean getting both the vehicle you need, and a driver; all at a reasonable fare. However, we also offer cars for you to drive yourself!

Guiding Services


You definitely want your tour to feel special and a tour-guide is the key. While planning, they may assist you with recommendations of places to go, as well as experiences you’re not supposed to miss on your trip around Western Uganda. Some of them double as drivers and they’re to answer any questions you have on our history, nature, cultures, people and places. Book yourself one of our guides now. You can expect well-educated tourism professionals who each have specialties and can transform your trip into a "Once-in-a-lifetime-Experience". Our guide charge is at UGX 100k/per day; one for each group of 1-6 people.
